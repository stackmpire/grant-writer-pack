FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know how to code?
No. You need Claude Code installed (free desktop app) and Python 3, which comes pre-installed on Mac. Setup takes about 5 minutes following the README. After that it's just typing slash commands — no code ever.
Do my past proposals get uploaded anywhere?
Never. The boilerplate vault stores and searches your proposals entirely on your local machine. Nothing is sent to any server. This is one of the core reasons to use a Claude Code plugin over a cloud SaaS — your client data stays yours.
Does it cover foundation grants, not just federal?
Version 1 is federal-only via grants.gov (which covers all US federal opportunities for free). Private foundation data from Candid/Instrumentl is on the roadmap but requires a paid data subscription. For federal-first writers, this covers the majority of opportunities.
How is this different from Instrumentl?
Instrumentl wins on private foundation data (490K+ funders). We win on price (4× cheaper), privacy (your proposals never leave your machine), and the RFP-to-skeleton drafter which Instrumentl doesn't offer. If you're federal-first and value data privacy, we're a better fit.
What's the 30-day money-back guarantee?
If the scanner doesn't surface at least one opportunity you hadn't already found in your first 30 days, reply to your welcome email and I'll refund you in full. No forms, no questions.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Reply "cancel" to any email and I'll stop the subscription the same day. No retention flows, no cancellation surveys. Your data stays on your machine regardless.
