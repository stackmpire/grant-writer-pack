Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: We collect only your email address (to deliver your license key) and your subscription info (to manage billing). Your proposals, grant data, and all files processed by the plugin never leave your machine — they are never sent to us or any third party.

1. Who we are

Grant Writer Pack ("we," "us," "our") is operated by an individual developer. You can reach us at fawazmpire@gmail.com.

2. What information we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

  • Email address — when you purchase a subscription, so we can deliver your license key and send you onboarding emails.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by our payment processor (Stripe or Lemon Squeezy). We never see, store, or have access to your credit card number, CVV, or full card details. We receive only a subscription status confirmation.
  • License key — a randomly generated key stored in our database, linked to your email address and plan, so we can verify your subscription and handle cancellations.
  • Subscription status — whether your subscription is active, so we can revoke access if a subscription is cancelled or refunded.

3. What we do NOT collect

The Grant Writer Pack plugin runs entirely on your local machine. The following data stays on your machine and is never transmitted to us or any server we operate:
  • Your past proposals or boilerplate documents
  • Your grant search results or ranked opportunities
  • Your profile.json configuration (keywords, agencies, award ranges)
  • Any generated draft proposals
  • Your grants.gov API results

We do not use cookies, analytics scripts, tracking pixels, session recording, or any third-party analytics tools on this website.

4. How we use your information

  • To deliver your license key after purchase.
  • To send onboarding and check-in emails (you may opt out by replying "unsubscribe" to any email).
  • To verify that your subscription is active when you contact support.
  • To process refunds if requested within the 30-day guarantee window.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Payment processing

All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. (or Lemon Squeezy, depending on your region). These processors are PCI DSS Level 1 certified, which is the highest level of payment security certification. Your card data is encrypted and transmitted directly to the payment processor — it never passes through our servers.

Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

6. Data retention

We retain your email address and license key for as long as your subscription is active. When you cancel, we retain your email for up to 90 days to handle any refund requests, then delete it from our records upon request.

To request deletion of your data, email fawazmpire@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request."

7. Third-party services the plugin contacts

When you run /grants-scan, the plugin makes network requests on your machine to:

  • grants.gov public API (api.grants.gov) — a US federal government public database. No authentication or personal data is sent; it's a plain search query. Grants.gov's privacy policy: grants.gov/privacy-policy

No other external services are contacted by the plugin unless you configure optional integrations (e.g., Notion sync) in your profile.json.

8. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect the data we do store: encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls on our license database, and no storage of payment credentials.

Because most of the product runs locally on your machine, the security of your proposal data depends on the security of your device — not our servers.

9. Children's privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify active subscribers by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The updated date at the top of this page always reflects when it was last revised.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled: fawazmpire@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
